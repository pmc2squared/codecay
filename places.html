Riding Unicorns and Snow Leopards

Riding majestic creatures is a unique way to kick off the day or end with a bang. This is usually a favorite winter or fall activity rather than summer or spring. The cost for riding an animal is $20 dollars for a full day of leaping and jumping around. You ride across the beautiful lands of Code Cay island and see all the special and historic landmarks the island has to offer.

Swimming with Mermaids

Kids usually love swimming with these amazing, mind-blowing, aquatic creates in the summer time. Swimming with mermaids has been an all time favorite activity on the island for many years. Swimming with one personal mermaid for an hour cost $30 dollars. Swimming with a pod of mermaids for the whole family to enjoy at once will cost you $75 dollars.

Photography Under the Sea

If photography is a hobby of yours, then you are in luck as the island offers this amazing activity. The price for one day with a waterproof camera included with an instructor, if needed will cost you $100 dollars. This activity is mostly enjoyed in the Spring, Summer, and Fall seasons.

Dancing on Water

Are you ready to whip, dab and pump it up in sytle? This year Code Cay has a new and improved activity, dancing on water! This is usually a two day session, cost being $50 dollars per day. This is a Summer and Spring activity, which many adults enjoy, and kids are welcome as long as they fit the height requirement and adult supervision.

Flying in the Sky

Flying is an interesting and creative activity on the island that stirs up fun for all. Fly around with different creatures in the air. The cost for half a day of flying will cost you $150 dollars for two, and it includes an instructor. This is a year-round activity that is available all year round.
